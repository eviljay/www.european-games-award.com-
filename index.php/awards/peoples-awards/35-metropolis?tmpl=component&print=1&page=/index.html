Summary Metropolis Crimes is a DS game which once again requires you to test out your investigative talents. You take control of Red Johnson, private detective, who investigates murders looking for the guilty party. Through mini-games, questions and answers and paying attention, you will get to the end of your investigation without having to think too hard. This is to be played as if you were living through a television series which you enjoy sitting down to on Sunday evening.
