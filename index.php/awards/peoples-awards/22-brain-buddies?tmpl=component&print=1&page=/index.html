Summary Brain Buddies offers its users a playful way to determine their brain weight. The game is focusing on a contest among friends to find the one with the heaviest brain. A large set of mini games as well as entertaining graphics make the game fun to play for a long time. 6 month after being launched, Brain Buddies has been played by over 10 million people from 220 countries, all together over 1 billion minutes of play time so far.
