Gamesload European Newcomer Award

With the Gamesload European Newcomer Award your team has the chance to win 2.500 € as prize money as well as a European-wide distribution deal with Gamesload if you achieve the first place.
Here are the guidelines for a successful submission to the award:

 

  • Your game has been developed by university students, was a student’s project or was created and developed by a team of independent developers.
  • Your team members are older than 16 years and have not yet worked in a professional game developer studio or as freelancer in the games industry.
  • Your project is not a commissioned work for a professional developer and has not yet been released in any distributional channels.
  • Your project is not encumbered with third persons’ rights, you own all rights to your project.
  • Your project must at least exist in a playable version (beta version, prototype) as a whole or in parts (levels), which gives an idea of the game’s concept. You may also include trailers, videos or screenshots to complete the game’s impression
  • The entry deadline for a successful submission is July 26th 2010.
  • Awardees can be students, young professional developers, branch newcomers, natural persons or legal entities, it they are authorized to accept the award for its proper use.
  • We reserve the right to cancel your submission to the award without further explanation even if all guidelines have been kept.
  • Your project must have been developed for the PC.
