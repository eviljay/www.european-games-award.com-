Summary Imagine a whole world where you could play, explore and create whatever you like - and imagine that you could take that world with you wherever you go. That's LittleBigPlanet PSP - the portable version of last year's iconic PS3 hit. Get inside the mind of the loveable Sackboy and explore, create and share - running, swinging and leaping through a universe where you can move everything you touch.
